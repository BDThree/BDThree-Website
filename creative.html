03 · Creative Practice

AI as
collaborator.

I am not a professional musician. I am a professional who makes music, which turns out to be a different and more interesting thing. Every song starts with something true: a person, a moment, a feeling that has been living long enough to need an exit. I work with AI to give those songs a production quality I couldn't otherwise access. Think of it the way a writer thinks about a printing press. The technology doesn't author the book. It gives the words a form that can travel.
