I build digital platforms that create real-world connection, between donors and the communities they serve, between audiences and the stories that matter to them.

At World Vision Canada, I led the development of an industry-first AI-powered donor engagement platform, connecting 100,000+ supporters to 700+ global projects through personalized impact reporting at scale. Before that, I spent over a decade at CBC/Radio-Canada building digital audio products heard by millions, launching Canada's first podcast ad server, and integrating AI-driven personalization long before it became standard practice.

What you'll also find here, if you keep scrolling: a creative practice that takes that same belief about human connection and runs it through a completely different machine. I produce original music and video using AI as a genuine creative collaborator, and release it into the world on the same platforms I've spent my career building for others.
